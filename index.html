About us

A research partner for every stage of your postgraduate journey.

Dissertation Desk is a South African professional services business dedicated to helping postgraduate students, researchers, and academic institutions successfully navigate the complex demands of academic research. The business targets a clear and underserved gap: thousands of Honours, Masters, and PhD students who lack access to expert support in data analysis, research methodology, academic writing, Word formatting, and referencing.

We provide expert, ethical and premium research support to Honours, Masters and PhD students, researchers and institutions. From data analysis and methodology consultation to academic editing, formatting and referencing, we help researchers produce work they can be proud of.

Services

What we offer.

01

Data Analysis

We offer comprehensive data analysis services, including full analysis, advisory support, and training in both quantitative and qualitative research methods. Our expertise includes, but is not limited to, SPSS, Python, R, Atlas.ti, and other leading analytical tools.

02

Research Methodology, Literature Review, and Proposal Planning

We provide expert guidance throughout the research process, helping you develop a structured and effective approach to your study. Our services include research design, literature review support, proposal development, academic resource identification, and the ethical use of AI tools to enhance research quality and efficiency.

03

Word Formatting

We assist with professional document formatting or provide training to help you do it yourself. Services include thesis and dissertation formatting according to university specifications, table of contents generation, lists of figures and tables, heading structures, styles, pagination, appendices, and other advanced Microsoft Word features.

04

Referencing Support

We offer referencing assistance and training across major citation styles, including APA 7th Edition, Harvard, Vancouver, and Chicago. We also provide training in reference management software to help streamline your academic writing process.

The founder

Meet Caivil Ndobela, CEO & Lead Consultant.

Caivil Ndobela holds a Master's degree from the University of Pretoria, funded by the Mastercard scholarship program, with a history of cum laude. During his Honours studies, he was recognized as one of the top-performing students in the Research and Development module, demonstrating exceptional academic and research capabilities. With several years of research experience, Caivil has actively contributed to the scientific community through participation in both international and local conferences. He has attended more than four international conferences, earning a Best Research Poster Presentation Award, and has also presented his work at multiple national conferences.

Caivil has held leadership and mentorship roles within the research community, including serving as a President within the South African Society for Bioinformatics. In this capacity, he organized and delivered data analysis training and workshops for researchers across South Africa and other African countries, including Madagascar and Nigeria, helping to build research capacity and strengthen analytical skills. He has also demonstrated strong event leadership, chairing both an international symposium and a national symposium hosted in Cape Town and Gqeberha, respectively.

Professionally, Caivil has had many years of experience at the University of Pretoria, where he has supported undergraduate and postgraduate students in research methodology, data analysis, and scientific computing. Notably, his work has included offering Python and R programming workshops, developing educational and training materials, and providing editorial support for theses and dissertations. Through these roles, he has helped researchers strengthen their technical skills and successfully complete high-quality academic research.

FAQ

Questions, answered.

Do you write dissertations?

No. We guide, support, perform data analysis, train, and advise. We never write academic work on behalf of students.

Do you assist online?

Yes. We support researchers throughout South Africa and internationally through online consultations.

Which software do you support?

SPSS, Python, R, Atlas.ti, Excel, and other research tools.

Can you help with methodology?

Yes. We assist with research design, proposal planning, literature reviews, and methodological decisions.
